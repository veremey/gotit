Project Overview

50 years ago, two brothers-in-law began a business with nothing but $500 to their names. Combining their traditional values and forward-thinking mentalities, they focused on building relationships. While this led to steady business, there was a dire need for brand awareness. So, they approached us at Got IT Digital with the request to revamp their digital presence.

Website Design and Development

A visionary company like V&V Supremo deserved a visionary digital presence. Got IT Digital partnered seamlessly with their C-Suite and sales team to strategize a large-scale online manifestation that highlighted their pioneering work and celebrated their authority within the industry.

  • Built a comprehensive main website that focused on the brand story, products, and availability
  • Built accompanying food service websites with high content authority
  • Radical site speed optimization
  • Set up and management of advanced Google Analytics and Google Data Studio
  • Delivered monthly interactive reporting to C-Suite and National Sales Team

SEO

In order to prove V&V Supremo was the best in the business, we had to position them as such across all search platforms. Meticulous keyword and market research, multilingual search engine optimization, and heavy personalized internal training were just a few of the steps Got IT Digital took to position V&V Supremo as number one.

  • Owned every keyword related to their brand
  • 284 keywords in Google’s Top Three
  • 1164 owned keywords ranking on Google’s first page

Built and Strengthened an

  • Established and trained an internal team
  • Conducted one on one coaching sessions with key members of the team, including the chefs!
  • Created partnerships between V&V Supremo and PR agencies
  • Created partnerships between V&V Supremo and influencers
