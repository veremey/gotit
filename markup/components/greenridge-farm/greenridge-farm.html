Website Design and Development

Greenridge Farm prides itself on handcrafting all its products, which made them the perfect match for us as partners. Building a website from scratch, we were able to create, host, and maintain the online platform upon which Greenridge Farm built itself. Most importantly, we were able to implement a “Store Locator” function that allowed for customers to easily find products and provide data for online market research.

SEO

A smart marketer knows that a brand’s digital presence is only as good as the SEO it builds for itself. The best marketer knows that it’s not just about being number one in a search engine. It’s about creating a deliberate brand narrative and bringing it to life online.

Got IT Digital worked with Greenridge Farm to translate its brand values, products, and key points across all search engines. Greenridge Farm was positioned as an expert for topics like “handmade”, “artisanal”, “non-GMO”, “lower nitrates”, and more.

It’s simple. Our SEO efforts made their brand look good in all the right places.
